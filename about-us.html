About us.


Composting is a great way to give back to the environment and help reduce our waste by ensuring that it's properly remediated. Composting is something that is more readily undertaken at home and less so in workplaces, so the idea behind Organic Matters is to make composting more accessible to the masses.

Organic Matters environmental services was conceived in 2017 by two friends who both have a passion to make a positive contribution to the environment. In our first year of operation we have diverted over half a tonne of food waste from landfill, which is a fantastic result for us.

Through a small change, our customers have made a big difference in their impact on the environment.
