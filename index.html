Small change, big difference

Helping you to bring composting to your workplace.

Learn more


All you need to do is seperate organic waste.


We take care of the rest.

We’ll come to your business for a meet and greet. From here we’ll assess your requirements and install our bins accordingly.

We’ll send you an information pack so that you can send out to your business regarding the do’s and don’t for our bins. You’ll then be ready to begin composting at your workplace.

Depending on your requirements we’ll come and collect the waste each week and give the bin a freshen up.

The waste product will be then taken back to our premises where we’ll let nature do its thing and turn your waste into reusable product.

A little bit about us



Composting is a great way to give back to the environment and help reduce our waste by ensuring that it's properly remediated. Composting is something that is more readily undertaken at homes and less so in workplaces, so the idea behind Organic Matters is to make composting more accessible to the masses.

Organic Matters environmental services was first conceived in 2017 by two friends who both have a passion to make a positive contribution to the environment. In our first year of operation, we have diverted over half a tonne of food waste from landfill, which is a fantastic result for us. Through a small change, our customers have made a big difference in how they impact the environment.
