We are Organic Matters

A Melbourne based waste management solution providing a food waste recycling service to workplaces & buisnesses in Melbourne.

Our Service

We deliver sexy compost bins to your workplace, we collect them once they're full and we make life-giving dirt to give back to nature.
It's simple. We do the dirty work for you.

Fact.


Currently in Australia, organic waste is one of the least recycled waste products with around 90% going directly to landfill. When disposed of, it decomposes and generates methane, a gas that is at least twenty-one times more potent than carbon dioxide in our atmosphere.

Fact.


A person in Australia generates 133kgs of food waste per year which is equivalent to the CO2 emmisions it takes to power an average household for 22 days.

(Australian National Waste Report, 2016)

Fact.

If food waste was a country, it would be the third biggest emitter of greenhouse gases after the USA and China.

(Food wastage footprint report, 2013)

Fact.

Food waste makes up to 40% of the average Australian household garbage bins.

Australian National Waste Report, 2010

Fact.

Australian consumers throw away around 3.1 million tonnes of edible food a year.

(Department of the Environment and Energy)

Fact.

Up to a third of all food is estimated to be wasted each year.

(FAO, 2013)

All you need to do is separate your organic waste, we take care of the rest


Here's how it works

We’ll come to your business for a meet and greet. From here we’ll assess your requirements and install our bins accordingly.


We’ll send you an information pack so that you can send out to your business regarding the do’s and don’t for our bins. You’ll then be ready to begin composting at your workplace.


Depending on your requirements we’ll come and collect the waste each week and give the bin a freshen up.


The waste product will be then taken back to our premises where we’ll let nature do its thing and turn your waste into reusable product.

About us.


Composting is a great way to give back to the environment and help reduce our waste by ensuring that it's properly remediated. Composting is something that is more readily undertaken at homes and less so in workplaces, so the idea behind Organic Matters is to make composting more accessible to the masses.

Organic Matters environmental services was conceived in 2017 by two friends who both have a passion to make a positive contribution to the environment. In our first year of operation we have diverted over half a tonne of food waste from landfill, which is a fantastic result for us.

Through a small change, our customers have made a big difference in how they impact the environment.
